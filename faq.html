Frequently Asked Questions

      

Quick answers to everything about NightShift AI

    
  
     
    
      

What exactly is NightShift AI?

      

        NightShift AI is a 24/7 voice assistant that answers all calls for your hotel,         handles bookings, quotes rates, screens emergencies, manages guest requests,         and instantly sends alerts to your phone.       

    
    
      

Does the AI sound robotic?

      

        No — NightShift AI uses natural speech with human pacing, emotion,         and clarity. Callers believe they are speaking to a real front-desk employee.       

    
    
      

Can it actually book rooms?

      

        Yes. It checks availability in real time, collects guest details,         calculates totals, and saves the reservation into your dashboard.         You receive an instant SMS + email summary for every booking.       

    
    
      

Is it compliant with laws like PCI & ADA?

      

        100%. The system strictly follows:         
• PCI – never accepts card numbers over the phone           
• ADA – follows service animal questions exactly           
• State hotel laws           
• Brand quality standards         

    
    
      

Does it replace or support my front-desk employees?

      

        Both. Most hotels use NightShift AI for low-volume hours (6pm–7am)         or overflow calls; others run it 24/7. It captures every call instantly         so you never lose bookings.       

    
    
      

How fast does it answer calls?

      

        Every call is answered in under 3 seconds, 24 hours a day.       

    
    
      

What happens during emergencies?

      

        NightShift AI immediately tells callers to hang up and dial 911.         Afterward it can transfer to the manager or front desk number on file.       

    
    
      

Can I review call logs?

      

        Yes. Every call is saved automatically — with transcripts, summaries,         caller ID, tags, sentiment, and reservation outcomes — inside your dashboard.       

    
    
      

Can the AI speak other languages?

      

        Yes. It automatically switches between English, Spanish, Hindi,         Gujarati, and French depending on what the caller starts speaking.       

    
    
      

How quickly can I start?

      

        Setup takes less than 10 minutes.           Most hotels go live the same day.       

    
    
      

Does NightShift AI integrate with PMS systems?

      

        Yes. Full PMS integrations are being released for Cloudbeds,         RoomMaster, Opera, and WebRezPro.           Until then, all reservations still log perfectly in your dashboard.       

    
    
      

What about staff notifications?

      

        Each reservation triggers instant:         
• SMS alert           
• Email summary           
• Dashboard entry         

    
    
      

How does billing work?

      

        Simple flat monthly pricing per property.           No contracts. Cancel anytime.
